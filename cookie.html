Our Cookies Policy explains what cookies are, how we use cookies, how third-parties we may partner with may use cookies on the Service, your choices regarding cookies and further information about cookies.

What are cookies

Cookies are small pieces of text sent by your web browser by a website you visit. A cookie file is stored in your web browser and allows the Service or a third-party to recognize you and make your next visit easier and the Service more useful to you.

Cookies can be "persistent" or "session" cookies.

How The Hermitage on Gozo uses cookies

When you use and access the Service, we may place a number of cookies files in your web browser.

We use cookies for the following purposes: to enable certain functions of the Service, to provide analytics, to store your preferences, to enable advertisements delivery, including behavioral advertising.

We use both session and persistent cookies on the Service and we use different types of cookies to run the Service:

Essential cookies. We may use essential cookies to identify browser sessions.

Third-party cookies

In addition to our own cookies, we may also use various third-parties cookies to report usage statistics of the Service, deliver advertisements on and through the Service, and so on.

Google Analytics uses first-party cookies to distinguish unique users, remember traffic source information, determine the start and end of a session, remember the value of visitor-level custom variables and throttle request rates.

What are your choices regarding cookies

If you'd like to delete cookies or instruct your web browser to delete or refuse cookies, please visit the help pages of your web browser.

Please note, however, that if you delete cookies or refuse to accept them, you might not be able to use all of the features we offer, you may not be able to store your preferences, and some of our pages might not display properly.

Where can your find more information about cookies

Terms and Conditions

The use of The Hermitage Suzuki Samurai is available for use of guests that stay for more than six nights unless otherwise discussed.

The vehicle is included in the price of The Hermitage, not including fuel.

Guests who wish to use the vehicle must provide a valid drivers license and be over 21. They must also sign an agreement on arrival prior to using the vehicle

The Suzuki is to be used on the island of Gozo only, taking the vehicle on the Gozo Channel ferry invalidates the insurance agreement on the car.

The vehicle is provided for your enjoyment and convenience. Please help us by returning the car with a full tank of fuel, and report any issues immediately.
