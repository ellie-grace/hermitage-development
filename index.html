The Hermitage

Holiday accommodation situated on the beautiful island of Gozo.

Welcome to Gozo

One of the last true gems of the Mediterranean. Whether you are a small family or group of friends and are looking for a cosy, two bedroom place to stay when visiting our island, then The Hermitage is made for you.

Within walking distance of Qala, this remote restored farmhouse in the idyllic countryside will give you all the peace you need. Enjoy your morning coffee at sunrise or a glass of wine at sunset, all with great views, all year round.

Amenities

Your home away from home has everything you will need to enjoy your holiday.

  • Living area with FlatScreen TV and fireplace
  • Fully equipped kitchen with Fridge Freezer, Microwave, Toaster and Gas Oven.
  • Separate dining room
  • Utility room with Toilet and Washing Machine
  • Main bedroom with king size bed and sea views with AC
  • Second bedroom available as twin singles or double bed with AC
  • Family bathroom located on first floor
  • Terrace with sea views
  • Outside dining and BBQ areas
  • Towels and linen provided
  • Electricity, gas and AC included
  • Free wi-fi throughout the property

Prices and Availability

The property is generally available on a weekly basis. We are happy to offer flexible arrival and departure dates when possible. Please take a look at the calendar below for availability and contact us directly for booking options, or more information.

Prices are dependent on the season and length of stay. If you book for six or more nights the following services are also included:


  • *Free use of the Suzuki Samurai for the duration of your stay.

  • Airport pick up and drop off if required. (Or accomodation on Malta)

  • Return ferry tickets between the mainland and Gozo.
Where to find us

The Hermitage is only a 10 minute drive from the Gozo ferry, and one and a half hours journey from Malta International Airport.
